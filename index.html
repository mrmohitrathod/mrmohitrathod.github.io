Hello Guys, welcome to the foodies on street, today we are not going to talk about street food. We want to share our feelings to you, we know there is lot of going are due to COVID-19 and as a responsible citizen of India we have to do what our government says for our own safety and our loved ones so we thought lets share some points with you.
First let's take a moment and salute to our heroes who are out there for us and fighting day and night.
So guys in this pandemic situation we don't have to be frightened we need some patience and have to relax for a while. Don't act like its not gonna end.

Everyone Should Do

Help those who are in need right now. Help elderly people and don't let them go out.
There is currently no vaccine to prevent coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19). The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to this virus. The virus is thought to spread mainly from person-to-person.
Clean your hands often. Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds especially after you have been in a public place.
Avoid any kind of close contact with people who are sick. Stay at home as much as possible.
Maintain Social distance and avoid crowding
Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth with unwashed hands. Clean AND disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily. This includes tables, doorknobs, light switches, countertops, handles, desks, phones, keyboards, toilets, faucets, and sinks.
Cover your mouth and nose with a cloth face cover when around other
So, guys, we hope you follow the instructions and stay safe till all this end. We have to stand as one and fight against this situation till then stay healthy and safe.
